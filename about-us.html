This is Who we are...

Raj Software Technology (India) Ltd. (RSTIL) is a technology-driven, professionally managed & well diversified IT Company having a definite & competitive edge in the field of IT training. We are committed to high, steady, and sustainable growth, excelling in business model, encompassing core areas of Information Technology.

RSTIL has its brand name registered as Raj Computers Academy (RCA) for IT training. RSTIL has 100 plus operational Franchisee Centres in Maharashtra, Gujarat, rajasthan and Bihar running under the name of Raj Computers Academy, and affiliated Centres in Sri Lanka, Bangladesh and UK. RCA has provided training to more than 400,000 students in the past 18 years. RCA is always striving to keep abreast with latest trends in the IT Education field. RCA offers wide range of IT training solutions with the latest training methodologies.

The Career Program list of RCA include SPA (Smart Professional Accountant), SMP (Smart Multimedia Professional), SIP (Smart IT Professional), G-Tech (Raj certified IT Graduate Program), Computer Hardware & Networking Program, these Programs offer varied opportunities in the IT Field. RCA provides Job Assistance in Case of the Career Programs. RCA offers short term programs in Basic Computer Operations, Desk-Top Publishing, Web Designing, Diploma in Digital Arts, Computerized Accounting with Tally.ERP9.

With the fast growing IT industry in India today there is a high demand for private institutes offering IT education. With the increase in the population, new geographies, number of youngsters opting for IT, there is huge scope for IT education business. There are only few IT education companies who are offering computer training programs to cater this need. The gap between the demand for IT education and number of companies offering IT education is big. The IT education business is very dynamic and any company to survive and remain in market should be abreast with latest technologies and happenings in the IT world. With the experience of past 18 years RSTIL is offering IT education in the form of excellent training modules, state of art training centres, professional team of faculties, latest program guides and study materials, CBTs etc. With its long & rich experience in IT Training RSTIL is the best strategic Business partner for an individual/company willing to venture into computer training by starting franchisee centre of Raj Computers Academy.
